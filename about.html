About Stumped?

Local service built on careful work, honest pricing, and long-term relationships in Snohomish County.

Locally owned and owner-operated

Stumped? is a local business focused on serving homeowners throughout Snohomish County and nearby communities. We’ve grown through word of mouth by showing up on time, communicating clearly, and leaving properties clean. Our work is grounded in practical experience with the soil, slopes, and access challenges common in the Pacific Northwest.

We keep the process straightforward: a clear quote, a professional grind, and a tidy finish. Whether you have a single stump in a small yard or multiple stumps after a removal project, we treat your property with care and respect.

What we believe in

  • Honest pricing without surprises
  • Protecting lawns, gardens, and hardscape
  • Communication that keeps you in the loop
  • Work that makes your property safer and more usable

Our commitment to homeowners

We know that homeowners want a safe, usable yard with minimal disruption. We plan access carefully, protect landscaping, and keep the work site clean. The result is a finished space that’s ready for sod, planting, or outdoor projects.

Because we serve a focused set of cities, we can respond quickly and schedule efficiently. Our primary service areas include Monroe, Duvall, Gold Bar, and Carnation, with regular work in Issaquah, Lake Stevens, Marysville, Bothell, and Woodinville.
