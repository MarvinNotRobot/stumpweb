Stump Grinding Services for Snohomish County

Clean, professional removal so you can replant, rebuild, and enjoy a smoother yard.

Full-service stump grinding

Our primary service is complete stump grinding for residential and light commercial properties. We grind below grade to remove trip hazards, allow for sod or landscaping, and keep pests away. We handle both single stumps and multiple stumps after a tree removal project.

Surface roots and problem areas

Surface roots can push up lawns, crack edging, and interfere with drainage. We can grind visible surface roots around the stump area to create a smooth, workable base.

Cleanup options

Grinding produces mulch that can be used on-site or hauled away based on your preference. We leave work areas clean and provide guidance for replanting, seeding, or future hardscape.

Residential stump removal

Ideal for homeowners who want a safe, level yard. We plan access for side yards, gates, and narrow driveways without damaging lawns.

Light commercial properties

We help small commercial sites maintain clean exteriors, particularly where visibility and safety are a priority.

Storm cleanup support

After wind or ice storms, stumps are often left behind. We coordinate with your tree removal contractor or handle stand-alone stump grinding.

What to expect

  • Clear, upfront pricing based on stump size and access
  • Schedule options for weekdays and limited weekend slots
  • Protected surfaces and a tidy finish
  • Optional advice on sod, seed, or replacement plantings
