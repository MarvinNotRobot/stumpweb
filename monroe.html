How our stump removal process works

We start with a quick phone quote based on stump size, quantity, and access. When we arrive, we confirm the work area, flag anything that needs protection, and explain the cleanup plan. We grind the stump below grade so the visible portion and surface roots are removed. The resulting mulch is either left in place for on-site use or hauled off, depending on your preference. We then tidy the site and walk you through next steps for re-seeding or landscaping.

Our goal is to make the job easy for Monroe homeowners. We keep communication clear, show up when we say we will, and leave the property clean. If you’re coordinating with a tree removal company, we’re happy to work around their schedule and handle the stump phase quickly afterward.
