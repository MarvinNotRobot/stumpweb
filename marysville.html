Preparing your Marysville yard

Clearing the area around the stump helps us work efficiently. If the stump is near a fence or shed, please move items that could block access. We also recommend marking irrigation lines or landscape lighting so we can protect them during grinding.

Seasonal considerations

In the wet months, softer ground can affect access, especially in low-lying yards. We plan equipment routes to avoid ruts and can schedule during drier windows when possible. During summer, we manage dust and keep the work area tidy for neighboring homes.
